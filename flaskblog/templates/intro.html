Welcome to Trading Futures Indexes

E-Mini Dow Jones Industrial Average

E-minis are futures contracts that represent a fraction of the value of standard futures. They are traded primarily on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange's Globex electronic trading platform and the New York Board of Trade.

Contract Specifications

The contract specifications for the YM mini futures market are as follows:

  • Symbol: YM
  • Expiration months: March, June, September, and December( H M U Z )
  • Exchange: Chicago Board of Trade(CBOT)
  • Currency: U.S. Dollar
  • Tick size/Minimum Price Fluctuation: 1.0 point
  • Tick Value or Profit/Loss Per Tick: $5.00
  • Ticks Per Point: One, making each point worth $5.00 per contract

Schedule

In general, the emini futures market is open for trading every trading day, Sunday night through Friday night. With the exception of holidays, trading here is limited to market open and early afternoon session. Typically, a position is held no longer than a few minutes, if that. No positions are held overnight. Hence, margin requirements vary.

Strategy

Style here is to scalp, catching small price movements, trading off of price charts. Each trading day is different. Method must work in nearly all market conditions.

Trading the YM is setup as entering either a buy stop, sell stop, or market order. Positions are bracketed with a target and stop loss typically of 20 points. This translates to a profit/loss of $100.00 per contract. This does not include commissions.

Growing Pains

Myself is my biggest enemy to success. Will I allow myself to win? Paper trading has no skin in the game. Hidden landmines along the journey have included overtrading, becoming too afraid to trade, chasing the market to recoup loss of last trade, not staying focused.

Strengths to draw upon: discipline, patience, adaptability, mental toughness, independence, forward thinking.
